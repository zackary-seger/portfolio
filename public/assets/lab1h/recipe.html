Zak's Fried Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 1 Potato
  • Olive Oil
  • Backyard BBQ Seasoning
  • Chilli Powder

Instructions

  1. Chop potato into thin slices using your favorite method
  2. Coat frying pan with olive oil and heat to medium/high
  3. Fry in pan about 5 minutes without seasoning and then add a generous amount of Backyard Seasoning
  4. Add 2 pinches of chilli powder and fry until finished (~15 minutes)
